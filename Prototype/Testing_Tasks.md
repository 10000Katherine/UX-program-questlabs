# QuestLabs Companion - Usability Testing Tasks

**Instructions for Participants**:  
Complete these 4 tasks using the QuestLabs Companion prototype. Take your time, think aloud, and be honest about your experience. There are no right or wrong answers - we want to learn from you!

---

## Background Scenario

You are a college student interested in tracking your personal growth, academic progress, and wellbeing. You've just discovered QuestLabs Companion, an app designed to help students like you stay accountable through daily reflections, goal setting, and peer support.

---

## Task 1: Complete Your First Daily Check-In

**Scenario**: It's 9 PM on a Tuesday. You've had a pretty good day - classes went well, you exercised, and spent time with friends. You want to record today's reflection before bed.

**Your Goal**: Complete a daily check-in in the app.

**Steps to try**:
1. Open the app and navigate to the daily check-in
2. Rate your mood, relationships, and academics for today
3. Select which activities you completed today
4. (Optional) Add a brief reflection note
5. Submit your check-in

**Success Criteria**: Your check-in is saved and you see confirmation

**Time Goal**: Complete in under 2 minutes

## Task 3: Set Up a New Goal

**Scenario**: You want to improve your fitness this semester. You decide to create a vision goal (3-year aspiration), a quarterly goal for this term, and weekly action steps.

**Your Goal**: Create a new goal in the three-tier goal framework.

**Steps to try**:
1. Navigate to the goals section
2. Explore the three levels: Vision Goals, Quarterly Focus, Weekly Quests
3. Try to add a new weekly quest (e.g., "Exercise 3 times this week")
